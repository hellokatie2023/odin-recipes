Curry

Description

A curry is a dish with a sauce seasoned with spices, mainly associated with South Asian cuisine.

Ingredients

  • Onion
  • Cilantro
  • Tomatoes
  • Vegetable oil
  • Fresh ginger root
  • White sugar
  • Coconut milk
  • Chicken stock
  • Curry powder

Steps

  1. 2 Tbsp olive oil.
  2. 1 small yellow onion, chopped (1 cup)
  3. 4 garlic cloves, minced (1 1/2 Tbsp)
  4. 1 Tbsp peeled and minced fresh ginger.
  5. 1 cup low-sodium chicken broth.
  6. 3/4 cup drained canned diced tomatoes or peeled, seeded, diced fresh tomatoes.
  7. Salt.
  8. Cayenne pepper, to taste.
