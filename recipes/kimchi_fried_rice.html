Kimchi Fried Rice

Description

Kimchi fried rice is easy, packed with flavor, customizable, and done in under 30 minutes, making it perfect for a busy weeknight.

Ingredients

  • Onion
  • Frozen vegetables
  • Eggs
  • Vegetable oil
  • Garlic
  • Kimchi
  • Kimchi juice
  • Day-old, cooked white rice
  • Kosher salt
  • Ground black pepper

Steps

  1. Scramble the eggs. Heat 1/2 tablespoon of vegetable oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Once the oil is hot, add the eggs and scramble them. Transfer the cooked eggs to a bowl and set them aside.
  2. Cook the vegetables. Add the remaining vegetable oil, onion, frozen vegetables, garlic, kimchi, and kimchi juice to the skillet and saut√© until the onion is translucent and softened, about 3 minutes.
  3. Cook the rice. Add the rice to the skillet, using a spatula or wooden spoon to break up any clumps. Reduce heat to medium-low, and continue to stir. Add the scrambled eggs back to the skillet and season with salt and pepper, then serve.
